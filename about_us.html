Notes App By Sushant Patil

Creating a To-Do list is one of the most effective ways for perfect time management. And always having it handy is just like “Sone pe Suhaga” so we made a software where anyone can add and handle their day to day tasks and always have it handy. People will no longer forget the things which they wanted to do in short or long term , they will always have a note of it with the deadline in our To-Do Master Website. By making this project we learnt various concepts in PHP like form validation , data insertion / data retrieval using database .
